# Acme Holdings — Q2 Cost-Base Review

## Executive summary

We reviewed Acme Holdings' Q2 cost base against the approved FY25 plan. Operating costs are approximately 4% above plan, driven mainly by contractor spend in the integration workstream. We recommend a phased reduction over the next two quarters rather than an immediate cut, to protect delivery milestones. EBITDA for the quarter is approximately $12.4M (source: management accounts, Q2 close), broadly in line with projections.
